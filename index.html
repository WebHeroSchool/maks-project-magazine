Dog finds a nice home

On MEmorial Day weekend, Bill and Mary Lou Johnson's daughter and son-in-law fell in love - with a dog. Andrew and Randi MAckintosh went to Shop and Share at Lilly Pulitzer, an event benefiting the Big Dog Ranch Rescue, in Wellingtomn. They played with a purebred Weimaraner, named Charlie, and decided to adopt him. He is adapting well to hisnew life. The Johnsons' other daughter, Erica, adopted a shar-pei mix the year before.
